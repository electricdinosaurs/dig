About

Dig creates a space for the community to come together and learn about the process of growing food and our food systems through hands-on learning. Our goal is to foster the relationship between nature, humans, and fresh, healthy food. ​All levels of experience and interest are welcome.

During non-COVID operations, ​"Dig-ins" occur every Sunday at 12PM. ​Dig-ins are work days that offer students the opportunity to learn hands on in the garden through instructions and tips from fellow members as well as occasional workshops from outside community leaders.

These workdays give students the chance to relax in-between studying and enjoy the beautiful climate of Los Angeles.

Location

Find us at the garden on the Mesa Lawn in Sunset Canyon Recreation Center (across from obstacle course).
